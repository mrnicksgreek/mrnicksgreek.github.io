Our Story

The authentic taste of Greece has arrived at Ocean Terminal.

Mr. Nick’s Greek Kitchen offers a food experience that is as close as possible to the real thing. All that might be missing is the sunshine! We offer traditional Greek gyros and Greek home cooking at its best! Our meals are cooked daily using fresh locally sourced meat and vegetables.

Meet Our Hosts

The host Angelo Melas and chef Nikolaos Deligiannis, both come from Epidaurus where many of the ingredients are sourced for the tavern. This includes the region’s awards winning olive oil, which happens to be pressed and bottled by one of Angelo’s relatives.

Nick has twenty-five years under his chef’s apron and Angelo has spent his career in the hospitality business in Toronto. They know their octapodi from their yiaourti!
